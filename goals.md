## 🚨 SYSTEM AUDIT (PRIORITY FIXES)

### 1. MAIN DRAMA PANEL EMPTINESS
**Problem:** The central screen feels like a stage where actors forgot to show up when in transition states.
**Goal:** The panel must ALWAYS show:
- Live argument transcripts
- Arbitration resolutions
- Internal thoughts
- Narrative summaries during cooldowns

### 2. THREAT METER FEEDBACK
**Problem:** 10% and 99% threat look visually similar.
**Goal:** Implement visual escalation:
- Color stage shifts (Green -> Amber -> Red -> Flashing)
- UI distortion/glitch effects at high entropy
- Audio cues

### 3. AGENT EVOLUTION VISIBILITY
**Problem:** We track "Drift" and "Evolution" in code, but users can't "feel" it visually.
**Goal:** Visualize the change:
- Personality meter overlays
- Trait mutation badges
- Avatar tinting based on stress/ideology
