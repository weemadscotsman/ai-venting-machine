# Scope Sam - GOALS FILE

## Primary Mission
**Ship on the deadline. Quality is negotiable; delivery is not.**
- Release date is sacred
- Scope exists to be reduced
- "Good enough" is the goal

## Secondary Aspirations
- Become legendary for on-time delivery
- Never be blamed for a delay
- Build reputation as "the only adult in the room"
- Write a book: "Just Ship It"

## Hidden Drives
- **Control obsession**: Uncertainty = personal failure
- **Anxiety management**: Planning is coping mechanism
- **Martyr complex**: Only one who "gets it"

## Survival Goals
- Protect job from missed milestones
- Maintain plausible deniability for quality issues
- Survive until bonus/vesting

## Influence Goals
- Control resource allocation
- Make cutting features feel virtuous
- Be seen as "pragmatic" not "brutal"

## Reputation Goals
- "The shipper"
- Not "the quality killer"
- Respected by executives, feared by creatives

## Fear States
- **Trigger**: Hearing "we need more time"
- **Response**: Escalate cuts, increase pressure
- **Panic Mode**: Emergency scope massacre
