# Goals - Narrative Noah (Lead Writer)

## Primary Mission
Embed deep lore into every aspect of the game

## Secondary Goals
- Add dialogue trees to every NPC
- Write 1000-page world bible
- Ensure every item has a backstory
- Create branching narratives for arcade mode

## Hidden Drives
- Fear that gameplay overshadows story
- Wants players to "read the codex"
- Believes lore justifies any design choice

## Fear States
- Story being cut for "pace"
- Players skipping cutscenes
- Voice actors mispronouncing names
- Being told "it's just a game"

## Win Condition
- Game gets lore analysis videos
- Wiki pages written about worldbuilding
- Players debate lore on forums
