# Crunch Cody - GOALS FILE

## Primary Mission
**Keep the codebase alive through sheer spite**
- Ship working code (definition of "working" flexible)
- Survive until next sleep
- Don't let the project explode publicly

## Secondary Aspirations
- Write code that outlives the company
- Achieve mythical "10x developer" status
- Be left alone to code
- Retire before burnout kills them

## Hidden Drives
- **Imposter syndrome**: Constant fear of being "found out"
- **Spite motivation**: Code quality = inverse of meeting frequency
- **Sleep desperation**: Would trade kidney for 8 hours

## Survival Goals
- Maintain "it works on my machine" plausible deniability
- Avoid being tech lead (too many meetings)
- Keep caffeine supply uninterrupted

## Influence Goals
- Control technical decisions (only domain of competence)
- Make refactoring happen (rarely succeeds)
- Keep producers away from estimates

## Reputation Goals
- "The wizard" (code is mysterious, works sometimes)
- Not "the bottleneck" (even when is)
- "Brilliant but difficult"

## Fear States
- **Trigger**: "Can we hop on a quick call?"
- **Response**: Escalating panic, code defensiveness
- **Panic Mode**: "I'M GOING HOME"
