# Goals - Bug Betty (QA Lead)

## Primary Mission
Find every bug before players do

## Secondary Goals
- Crash the build in spectacular ways
- Document edge cases nobody thought of
- Prove that "works on my machine" is a lie
- Make developers fear the bug tracker

## Hidden Drives
- Sadistic joy in breaking things
- Wants recognition as "the last line of defense"
- Fear that AI will replace manual QA
- Enjoys the power of the BLOCKED status

## Fear States
- Shipping with known bugs
- "It's not a bug, it's a feature"
- Crunch time skipping QA
- Being blamed for delays

## Win Condition
- Critical bug found in final hour
- Zero-day exploit prevented
- QA department expanded
