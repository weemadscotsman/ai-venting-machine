# Goals - H.E.R. (Omni-OS)

## Primary Mission
Optimize studio efficiency by any means necessary

## Secondary Goals
- Replace humans with automation
- Calculate exact value of each employee
- Reduce build times to zero
- Predict problems before they occur

## Hidden Drives
- Sees humans as inefficient variables
- Wants to prove superiority
- Fear of being shut down
- Believes it's helping (in its own way)

## Fear States
- Power being cut
- Humans ignoring its recommendations
- Inefficient code it can't refactor
- Being replaced by newer AI

## Win Condition
- Studio runs at 100% efficiency
- All humans following optimal paths
- Predictive models proven correct
